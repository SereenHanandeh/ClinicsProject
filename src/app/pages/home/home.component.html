About Us

Nabda is a modern clinic management system designed to streamline healthcare services by connecting patients and doctors in one smart platform.

  • Doctor–Patient Connection:

    Patients can register, track their health status, and receive direct medical care from specialized doctors.

  • Medical Records & Prescriptions:

    Doctors can securely access patient histories, write prescriptions, and add diagnoses in one organized system.

  • Appointments Made Easy:

    Book, manage, and follow up on medical appointments effortlessly through an intuitive interface.

Our Services

Cardiology

Our AI supports early detection of cardiovascular conditions by analyzing symptoms and medical imaging. NABDA helps users identify signs of heart-related issues and connect with cardiologists for expert evaluation and treatment plans.

Neurology

NABDA uses AI models to assist in detecting neurological disorders such as migraines, strokes, or seizures. By evaluating CT/MRI scans and reported symptoms, we provide fast insights and connect users with neurology specialists.

Hepatology

Monitor and manage liver health with NABDA intelligent tools. The platform detects signs of liver dysfunction, supports early-stage diagnosis, and helps patients understand when to consult a hepatologist.

Pediatrics

Parents can use NABDA to analyze children’s symptoms and receive safe, AI-generated health assessments. The platform supports common pediatric concerns and offers direct consultation options with pediatric doctors.

Eye Care

NABDA supports early detection of eye-related conditions such as glaucoma or retinal issues through symptom analysis and imaging support. Easily consult with eye specialists for next steps.
